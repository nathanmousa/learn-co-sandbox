How to commit: (Do this every 15 minutes.)
-----------------------------------------

Type 'git add .' <--- (Get all of your altered files ready to commit.)
Type 'git commit -m ‘commit message contents’' <--- (Commit them to git.)
Type 'git push'  <--- (Push the files to your github.)
